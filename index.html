About Me...

I'm Carie Sherman, and I'm a writer. I spent 12+ years in the corporate closet before coming out as a creative. Alas, all was not lost: I understand what happens on the client side of the desk. No matter what your project, ten bucks says I’ve muddled through something similar. A freelancer by choice, I write for a number of clients, including The University of Colorado School of Medicine, The Colorado Trust, and Info Cubic. I also write behind the scenes for a number of advertising/marketing agencies, including Agency Zero and Peak Creative Media. I love words more than I love stretchy pants and sweetcorn.
